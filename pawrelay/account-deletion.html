PawRelay Account Deletion

Last updated: June 11, 2026

PawRelay stores pet care records locally on your device in the current test build. If you use Family Sync sign-in, you can request deletion of your PawRelay sharing account and associated server-side sharing data.

How To Request Deletion

Send an email to hyunever100@gmail.com with the subject PawRelay account deletion request.

Please include the email address used for PawRelay sign-in and, if available, the user ID shown in the app.

In-App Entry Point

  1. Open Household.
  2. Sign in to the sharing account.
  3. Tap Request account deletion.
  4. Send the generated email request.

Local Device Data

Local-only app data can be removed by reinstalling PawRelay or using the app setup reset flow. Photos attached in the MVP are local to the device unless a sync feature explicitly says otherwise.
