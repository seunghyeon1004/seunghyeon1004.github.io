Common Questions

Does PawRelay sync across family members?
Not yet. The first release is local-first. Family sharing is planned after market validation.

Does PawRelay diagnose pet symptoms?
No. PawRelay records care and symptoms for owner reference. Contact a veterinarian for medical advice.

How do I delete my data?
Delete records in the app, reset setup where available, clear app storage, or uninstall PawRelay.

How do I restore Plus?
Use the restore purchase option with the Google Play account that purchased PawRelay Plus.
