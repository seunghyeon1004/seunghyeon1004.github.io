PawRelay Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2026

1. Who We Are

PawRelay is operated by PawRelay. You can contact us at hyunever100@gmail.com.

2. What PawRelay Does

PawRelay helps dog and cat households manage local care routines, records, symptoms, photos, refill tracking, reminders, and vet-summary notes. PawRelay does not provide veterinary diagnosis, medical advice, prescription advice, or emergency care.

3. Information You Enter In The App

You may choose to enter or create:

  • Caregiver name or nickname
  • Pet name, species, optional birth date, and optional pet profile photo
  • Routine tasks, care-completion history, skipped/snoozed task records, schedules, refill records, and local exports
  • Symptom notes, severity, timestamps, and care photos

In the first release, this information is stored locally on your device. PawRelay does not run a cloud account system, backend sync service, or server-side family sharing feature.

4. Photos And Device Permissions

If you use care photos or profile photos, PawRelay asks for camera or photo-library permission before accessing those features. Photos are copied into app-local storage where possible. You can delete photo records in the app, and you can remove the app from your device to delete local app data.

5. Advertising

The free version of PawRelay may show ads through Google AdMob. The Google Mobile Ads SDK may process data such as IP address, product interactions, diagnostic information, and device or advertising identifiers for advertising, analytics, and fraud-prevention purposes. Google states that data collected by the Google Mobile Ads SDK is encrypted in transit using TLS.

You can control or reset Android advertising identifiers through Android device settings where available. PawRelay Plus removes in-app banner ads from the paid experience.

6. Purchases

PawRelay Plus purchases are handled by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment card details. Google Play may provide purchase status information needed to unlock or restore Plus features.

7. Sharing

If you use the device share sheet to share a care photo, the selected photo is sent only to the app or service you choose. PawRelay does not control how that third-party app or service handles the shared file.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. In the first release, PawRelay does not transmit your pet records, symptom notes, care photos, or routines to a PawRelay server. Third-party SDKs such as Google AdMob and Google Play services may process data according to their own policies and SDK behavior.

9. Security

PawRelay stores app data locally on your device using platform storage. Because the first release is local-first and does not send pet records to a PawRelay backend, server-side encryption is not applicable. Network communication used by Google Play, AdMob, and operating-system services should use modern encrypted transport. We recommend protecting your device with a passcode, biometric lock, and current OS updates.

10. Retention And Deletion

Local app records remain on your device until you delete them, reset setup inside the app, clear app storage, or uninstall the app. If you contact us for support, we retain support messages only as long as needed to respond and maintain records required by law or platform policy.

11. Children

PawRelay is not designed for children and should be used by adults or with adult supervision. Do not enter information about children into PawRelay.

12. International Use

PawRelay may be available in multiple countries. Privacy and data-protection rights may vary by location. Contact us at hyunever100@gmail.com with privacy requests.

13. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as PawRelay changes. The effective date above will show when this policy was last updated.

14. Contact
