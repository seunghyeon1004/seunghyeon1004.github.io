Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Fox's After-School Lab uses TikTok integration only to support creator-authorized video publishing.

Information We Access

With permission, the integration may access basic TikTok account information needed to verify the connected account and may publish videos selected by the creator.

How Information Is Used

Access tokens are used only to call TikTok APIs for account verification and video publishing. Tokens are stored locally by the creator and are not sold or shared.

Data Retention

Local publishing records may include video paths, captions, privacy levels, and TikTok publish identifiers for operational tracking.

Revocation

The creator can revoke TikTok access at any time through TikTok account settings or by deleting the local token file.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the operator through the official Fox's After-School Lab channel.
